About Me

Hi, I’m Adnan Khan — a developer and AI enthusiast who enjoys turning ideas into real-world projects. I’m passionate about blending creativity with problem-solving and exploring how technology can create meaningful impact.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at LNCT Bhopal and am now pursuing my Master’s at IIT Bombay. Along this journey, I earned an All India Rank of 36 in Data Analytics and 143 in Computer Science in GATE — milestones that reflect my dedication and love for the field.

My long-term vision is to become a top expert in AI and to build products that combine technical depth with real-world value. Beyond academics and development, I’m endlessly curious — always looking to learn, explore new ideas, and challenge myself with meaningful projects.

This website is a glimpse into my journey so far. Thanks for visiting — feel free to connect if you’d like to collaborate!
